What do we offer?

We offer wellness services in a calm, relaxing environment that seeks to restore balance and encourage resilience to benefit personal and family life.

Ear Acupuncture

The service involves the gentle insertion of thing needles into the outer ear. The points are adopted from the substance-abuse protocol developed by Dr. Michael Smith, L.Ac., of the National Acupuncture Detox Association. It empowers people to tap into their own reserves for balance and resilience which builds and restores us at our deepest levels. It calms the nervous system, relieves stress and anxiety, builds energy, detoxifies and helps us leg go of what no longer serves. The duration of the treatment is individualized and can range from 5-30 minutes.

Zero Balancing®

A form of massaging, ZB balances energy flow in the muscle and bones. Developed by Physician, Osteopath and Acupuncturist, Dr. Fritz Smith, Zero Balancing® brings stability to the nervous system and supports healing by creating internal order and clarity. Sessions last 15-20 minutes.

Massage Therapy

Massage combines the art and science of touch, primarily through the hands-on manipulation of muscles and connective tissures of body to enhance function, increase flexibility and promote relaxation. Sessions last 15-20 minutes.

Guided Meditation

A practice involving the quieting of the mind to increase peace and calm and relax the nervous system. Sessions last 15-20 minutes.

Who do we serve?

Spouses and family members

The family is part of the back bone of our armed forces. We provide free integrative medicine for both parties, thus strengthening our military might. Our acupuncture treatments have been designed for a group setting, to facilitate partnership on the journey toward wellness.

Caregivers and Warrior Support Staff

We recognize the impact of long hours and the intensity of caring for our warriors. Through free, onsite, stress relieving treatment, we improve moral and overall health of those caring for our warriors without straining the resources of the hospital.

Service Members

Wherever a service member is in his/her military career i.e. pre or post deployment, new to the armed forces or 30 years-time in service. Source Wellness can assist in maintaining health of the mind/body and spirit. Source Wellness strengthens our military power with no cost to the service member or government.
